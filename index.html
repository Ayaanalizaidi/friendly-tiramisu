Our Services

Comprehensive Inspection Solutions

We offer end-to-end quality assurance services across global supply chains, ensuring your products meet international standards before shipment.

On-Site Inspection

Factory floor verification with trained inspectors ensuring products meet specification before shipment. We catch defects at the source.

Textile Quality Checking

Comprehensive fabric standards verification including fiber content, color fastness, shrinkage, tensile strength, and defect classification.

Garment / Fabric Inspection

Pre-shipment and in-line inspection for apparel including AQL sampling, measurement checks, workmanship, and packaging compliance.

Home Textile / Consumer Products

End-product compliance verification for home furnishings, soft goods, and consumer products including safety standard checks.

Support Auditing & Compliance

Factory social audits, quality management system verification, and regulatory compliance support for global brands and importers.

Testing Coordination

End-to-end testing coordination with accredited labs worldwide. We manage the process from sample submission to certified results.
